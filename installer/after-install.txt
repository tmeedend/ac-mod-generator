The installation is finished.

You will see a new action when you right click on a file called "Assetto Corsa Mod". This action will try to generate a mod from the files or directories selected.

The archive will be created on your desktop.

For the moment, you wil see the results in the appearing shell.

You can also use it as a command line to automatically generate your mods. You can see the README.md situed in the app installation directory.
